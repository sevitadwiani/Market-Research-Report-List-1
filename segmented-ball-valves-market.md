<p><p>Segmented ball valves are specialized valve types used for fluid control in various industrial applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves feature a resilient material lining that provides leak-tight sealing, making them suitable for services involving low to medium pressure and temperature. In contrast, hard seal valves utilize metal or ceramic components for enhanced durability and resistance to high temperatures and pressures, making them ideal for demanding environments.</p></p>
