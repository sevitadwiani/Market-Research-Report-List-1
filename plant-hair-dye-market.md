<p><p>The plant hair dye market consists of three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Semi-permanent dyes offer a longer-lasting color, typically fading after several washes, without harsh chemicals. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft, ensuring long-lasting color that remains until the hair grows out, appealing to those seeking a lasting change. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
