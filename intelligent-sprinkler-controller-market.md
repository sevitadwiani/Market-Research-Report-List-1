Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust watering schedules, ensuring efficient water usage by responding to temperature, humidity, and rainfall. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors to determine the precise needs of plants, activating irrigation only when necessary. Both types aim to optimize resource use, enhance landscape health, and promote sustainable water management in various settings.</p></p>
