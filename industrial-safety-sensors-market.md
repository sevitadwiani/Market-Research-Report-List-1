<p><p>The industrial safety sensors market includes various types designed to protect workers and ensure safe operations. Safety light curtains utilize invisible beams to detect objects and stop machinery when an intruder enters a hazardous area. Safety laser scanners employ laser beams to create a protective field, enabling flexible monitoring of environments and detecting potential collisions. Safety edges are tactile sensors that trigger a safety response when pressure is applied, commonly used on machinery and equipment to prevent accidents.</p></p>
