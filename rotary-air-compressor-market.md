<p><p>The Rotary Air Compressor market is primarily categorized into two types: Oiled and Oil-free rotary air compressors. Oiled rotary compressors use oil for lubrication, enhancing efficiency and cooling but may produce contaminants in the compressed air, making them suited for industrial applications where air purity is less critical. Conversely, oil-free rotary compressors utilize advanced technology to eliminate oil, ensuring clean air output, ideal for sensitive applications like pharmaceuticals and food processing, where air quality is paramount.</p></p>
