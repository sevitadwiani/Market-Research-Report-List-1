<p><p>The air supply system market can be categorized into low-pressure and medium-pressure segments. Low-pressure systems typically operate below 15 psi, suitable for applications like pneumatic tools and light industrial processes. They offer energy efficiency and ease of maintenance. Medium-pressure systems range from 15 to 100 psi, utilized in more demanding applications such as material handling and automation. These systems provide higher performance and greater control over pressure variations, making them ideal for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
