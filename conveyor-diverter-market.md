<p><strong>The Conveyor Diverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conveyor diverter market is primarily categorized into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal diverters facilitate the lateral flow of materials on a conveyor line, redirecting goods without changing their orientation. This type is efficient for sorting parcels or packages. In contrast, vertical diverters elevate or lower items, allowing for more versatile space management, especially in multi-level systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing material handling efficiency across various industries, from logistics to manufacturing, optimizing workflow and productivity.</p></p>
