<p><strong>The Polysilicon Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polysilicon wafer market is primarily divided into two types: P-Type and N-Type wafers. P-Type wafers are doped with elements like boron, which creates a positive charge carrier, making them widely used in the solar photovoltaic industry due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. N-Type wafers, doped with elements like phosphorus, offer higher efficiency and better performance in shading conditions, though they are generally more expensive. Together, these wafer types serve diverse applications in solar cells and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
