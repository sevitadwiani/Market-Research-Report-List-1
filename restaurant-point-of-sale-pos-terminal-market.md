<p><p>The Restaurant Point of Sale (POS) terminal market comprises several key types. Fixed POS terminals are typically installed at cash counters, enabling staff to process transactions efficiently. Self-serve kiosks allow customers to place orders and make payments independently, enhancing convenience. Vending machines represent a fixed POS system that automates sales of food or beverages without staff involvement. Meanwhile, mobile POS terminals use handheld devices, enabling on-the-go transactions for servers, enhancing customer service and operational flexibility across restaurant settings.</p></p>
