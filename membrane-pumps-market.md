<p><p>The membrane pumps market consists primarily of air-operated diaphragm pumps and electric-driven diaphragm pumps. Air-operated diaphragm pumps utilize compressed air to move fluids, making them suitable for hazardous or volatile environments. They offer flexibility and are easy to maintain. In contrast, electric-driven diaphragm pumps rely on electric power for operation, providing greater efficiency and control over flow rates. Both types are widely used in industries such as chemical processing, wastewater treatment, and pharmaceuticals for their reliable performance and versatility.</p></p>
