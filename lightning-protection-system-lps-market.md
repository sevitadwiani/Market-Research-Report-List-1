<p><strong>The Lightning Protection System (LPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Lightning Protection System</li><li>Non-Conventional Lightning Protection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lightning Protection System (LPS) market is categorized into two main types: Conventional and Non-Conventional systems. Conventional Lightning Protection Systems utilize grounded conductors, air terminals, and bonding techniques to direct lightning strikes safely to the ground. In contrast, Non-Conventional Lightning Protection Systems incorporate advanced technologies, such as early streamer emission and charge dissipation methods, to enhance safety and improve response times. Both types aim to protect structures and occupants from lightning-induced damage and enhance overall electrical safety.</p></p>
