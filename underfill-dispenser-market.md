<p><strong>The Underfill Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Flow Underfill</li><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underfill Dispenser market includes three primary types: Capillary Flow Underfill, No Flow Underfill, and Molded Underfill. Capillary Flow Underfill utilizes the capillary action to fill gaps between the chip and substrate, ensuring reliable thermal and mechanical performance. No Flow Underfill features a non-flowing material, offering better control over application but requiring precise placement. Molded Underfill combines molding techniques with underfill materials, providing enhanced protection and mechanical robustness, making it suitable for high-density packaging applications.</p></p>
