<p><p>Gravity filters are essential in separating solids from liquids based on density differences, featuring two main market types: vertical and horizontal gravity separators. Vertical gravity separators utilize a taller design to encourage faster settling of particulates, making them efficient for applications with limited space. Conversely, horizontal gravity separators offer a broader surface area, enhancing separation efficiency for larger volumes of fluid. Both types serve various industries, including wastewater treatment, oil and gas, and food processing, each tailored to specific processing needs.</p></p>
