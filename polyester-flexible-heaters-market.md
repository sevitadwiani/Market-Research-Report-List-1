<p><strong>The Polyester Flexible Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Etched Foil Polyester Flexible Heaters</li><li>Wire Wound Polyester Flexible Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyester Flexible Heaters market comprises two main types: Etched Foil Polyester Flexible Heaters and Wire Wound Polyester Flexible Heaters. Etched Foil heaters utilize a thin metal layer etched into a specific pattern to create heating elements, offering fine, uniform heat distribution. In contrast, Wire Wound heaters comprise a resistive wire wound in a spiral pattern, providing higher watt densities and robustness. Both types are lightweight, flexible, and can be tailored for various applications, including medical devices, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
