<p><p>The engine management sensors market includes various critical components that monitor and optimize engine performance. Camshaft and crankshaft position sensors track the timing of engine components, while oxygen sensors measure exhaust gases for optimal combustion. Manifold absolute pressure sensors assess air intake levels, and mass airflow sensors determine air volume for fuel efficiency. Coolant temperature sensors regulate engine temperature, and knock sensors detect engine vibrations to prevent damage. Together, these sensors enhance efficiency, reduce emissions, and improve overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
