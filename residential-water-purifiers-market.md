<p><p>The residential water purifiers market comprises primarily three types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers, UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers, and other systems. RO purifiers utilize a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved impurities and contaminants, making them suitable for areas with hard water. UV purifiers use ultraviolet light to disinfect water by killing bacteria and viruses, ideal for bacteriologically unsafe sources. Other purifiers may include carbon filters or multi-stage systems, offering varied filtration methods to ensure clean drinking water.</p></p>
