<p><p>The Military Grade 12-inch Silicon Wafer market is divided into lightly doped and heavily doped categories. Lightly doped wafers possess fewer impurities, providing higher resistivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications where precision and reliable electrical properties are crucial. Heavily doped wafers, on the other hand, contain more dopants, which decrease resistivity and enhance conductivity, suitable for applications requiring rapid charge carrier movement. Both types serve significant roles in military and aerospace electronics, ensuring efficient and robust performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
