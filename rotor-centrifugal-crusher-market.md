<p><p>The Rotor Centrifugal Crusher market features three main types based on their crushing mechanisms. The Rock on Rock Rotor Centrifugal Crusher utilizes a self-impact process where material collides with itself, resulting in a high-quality sand product. The Rock on Iron variant employs metal anvils to enhance fragmentation, offering durability and efficiency for harder materials. Meanwhile, the Rock on Anvil model combines both approaches, providing a versatile solution that balances impact and material durability, suitable for various aggregate applications.</p></p>
